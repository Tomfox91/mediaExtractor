MediaExtractor Bookmarklet

This bookmark shows the URLs of the media currently playing in your browser tab.

MediaExtractor

Usage

  1. drag the bookmarklet below to your bookmarks bar
  2. navigate to a page with media content
  3. start playback
  4. click the bookmarklet
  5. profit!
